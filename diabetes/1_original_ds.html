Introduction¶

The Diabetes 130-US hospitals dataset from the UCI Machine Learning Repository contains hospital admission records of diabetic patients from 130 U.S. hospitals (1999–2008). Sourced from Cerner Corporation's Health Facts® database, it includes demographics, lab results, medications, and outcomes. De-identified for privacy, it is valuable for diabetes research and predictive modeling, though hospital variability may impact generalizability. Each row concerns hospital records of patients diagnosed with diabetes, who underwent laboratory, medications, and stayed up to 14 days.

The goal of this dataset conducted by researchers was to determine the early readmission of the patient within 30 days of discharge.
